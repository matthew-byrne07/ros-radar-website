02 What Personal Data We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

A. Account & Identity Data

  • Full name and email address (provided at registration)

  • Profile metadata you choose to add (e.g. company name)

B. Receipt & Financial Data

C. Payment & Subscription Data

D. Technical & Device Data

  • Authentication tokens (session JWTs, managed by Supabase Auth)
  • App preferences (theme, tax bracket setting — stored locally on device via SharedPreferences)
  • Basic usage logs and error traces for debugging purposes (no receipt content is included in logs)

04 How Data Is Processed — Technical Pipeline

When you capture a receipt, the following processing steps occur:

Capture — Your device camera or photo library produces a raw image. The image is compressed on-device (JPEG, max 1920px, ~800KB) before any upload to reduce data transfer.

Secure Upload — The compressed image is transmitted over an encrypted TLS connection to Supabase Storage (EU-hosted, AWS EU-West-1, Dublin, Ireland). Each image is stored in a private, user-isolated storage bucket. No other user can access your files.

AI Extraction — A server-side Edge Function (Deno runtime, hosted on Supabase) is triggered by the upload. This function calls the OpenAI GPT-4o Vision API to extract financial data. The image URL is passed securely via server-to-server call; your device never communicates directly with OpenAI. We have configured our OpenAI integration to opt out of model training on submitted data.

Structured Storage — Extracted data (merchant name, date, amounts, VAT, category) is written to a PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase (EU-West-1). Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure only your account can query your own records.

Display — Structured data is returned to your device over an authenticated, encrypted API connection and rendered locally in the app.

06 Data Retention

Retention periods are determined by legal obligations and operational necessity:

Receipt images & extracted financial data

  • Retained for a minimum of 6 years from the end of the tax year to which the receipt relates, in compliance with Section 887 TCA 1997
  • Hard deletion within the 6-year statutory period is blocked at the database level

Account & identity data

  • Retained for the duration of your account plus 12 months after account closure, to allow dispute resolution
  • After 12 months post-closure, account data is permanently deleted unless a legal hold applies

Payment records

  • Transaction records are retained for 7 years in compliance with Irish Revenue and VAT obligations

Session tokens & authentication data

  • Active session tokens expire after 1 hour (Supabase default). Refresh tokens expire after 30 days of inactivity

Technical logs

  • Error and debug logs are retained for 30 days and contain no receipt content or financial amounts

07 Data Security

We implement the following technical and organisational security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 (enforced by Supabase/AWS)
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies at the database level ensure complete data isolation between users — no user can read, write, or query another user's records
  • Private storage bucket policies prevent direct access to receipt images without a valid authenticated session
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (managed by Supabase Auth — we never store plaintext passwords)
  • API access requires a valid JWT signed with your Supabase project secret
  • Edge Functions run in an isolated Deno environment with no persistent file system

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the Data Protection Commission within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay, as required by GDPR Article 33.

08 International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed within the European Economic Area (EEA) on AWS EU-West-1 infrastructure in Dublin, Ireland.

Where transfers outside the EEA occur (e.g. certain Stripe or OpenAI operations), we ensure these are protected by:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) incorporated into our processor agreements
  • The EU–US Data Privacy Framework (where applicable)
  • Adequacy decisions issued by the European Commission

No receipt images or financial records are routinely transferred outside the EEA.

09 Your Rights Under GDPR (Articles 15–22)

Right of Access (Art. 15)

You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.

Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. You can update your name and email directly in Profile & Settings.

Right to Erasure — "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17)

You may request deletion of your account and personal data. Note: financial records within the 6-year s.887 TCA 1997 statutory retention period cannot be deleted until that period expires, as deletion would constitute a legal breach. We will delete all data outside this obligation.

Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)

You may request that we restrict processing of your data while a dispute is being resolved.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

You may request an export of your expense records in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

Right to Object (Art. 21)

You may object to processing based on legitimate interests (e.g. marketing). We will cease that processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any right, email: privacy@rosradar.ie
We will respond within 30 days. No fee is charged for standard requests.

10 Cookies & Local Storage

ROS Radar is a native mobile application and does not use browser cookies.

We use the following local device storage mechanisms:

  • SharedPreferences — stores your theme preference (light/dark) and tax bracket setting locally on your device. No personal or financial data is stored here.
  • Supabase Auth session tokens — stored securely in the device keychain/keystore to maintain your authenticated session. These are cleared when you sign out.

No third-party analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or tracking pixels are embedded in this application.
